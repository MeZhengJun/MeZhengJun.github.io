英语基础语法-06行为动词

发表于 2017-12-06 更新于 2018-01-01 分类于 学习 阅读次数： 本文字数： 597 阅读时长 ≈ 1 分钟

行为动词(Action Verbs)表示一个动作，是最常见的动词。

需要在第三人称单数的时候尾部追加’s’

  • He $\underline{eats}$ bread.
  • She $\underline{walks}$ to the station.
  • It $\underline{floats}$ on the sea.

否定句型需要do not, does not, 或者did not

  • I do not eat bread.
  • He does not eat bread.
  • You did not walk to the station.
  • It does not float on the sea.

疑问句以do, does, 或did开始

  • Do you eat bread?
  • Does he eat bread?
  • Does she walk to the station?
  • Did they finish it?

Do not可以缩写为don’t, does not可以缩写为doesn’t, did not可以缩写为didn’t.

  • I don’t eat bread.
  • She doesn’t walk to the station.
  • It doesn’t float on the sea.
  • They didn’t finish it.

练习：修正句子中动词使用的错误
1)I runs a marathon.
2)You look sleepy.
3)She do not dance.
4)Does you leave today?
5)We don’t stay here.
6)It come with rice.
