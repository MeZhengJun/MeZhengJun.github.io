不规则动词（Irregular Verbs）

常规的动词过去式和过去分词形式是尾部加ed (d)

Base Verb Past Past Participle
learn learned learned
study studied studied
cook cooked cooked
solve solved solved
ask asked asked
watch watched watched
listen listened listened

非常规的动词虽然没有固定的规则，也有一些模式可以参考

bring
drink
think
tell
eat
make
beat

答案

brought – brought
drank – drunk
thought – thought
told – told
ate – eaten
made – made
beat – beaten
